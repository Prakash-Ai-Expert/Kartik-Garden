✦ Our Story ✦

A Legacy of Celebrations

Nestled in the heart of Badarpur, New Delhi, Kartik Garden is a grandeur venue that has been the cradle of unforgettable celebrations for over a decade. Our sprawling lawns and elegant halls echo with the laughter of thousands of happy families.

From intimate receptions to lavish baraat processions — we bring the full splendour of Indian wedding traditions to life, with contemporary comforts that make every guest feel like royalty.

All-Inclusive Services

From the first decoration to the last guest's farewell — we handle every detail.

🌸
Floral Decoration

Stunning marigold arrangements, rose mandaps, and custom floral arches that transport you to a palace of blooms.

🍽️
Royal Catering

Authentic Indian cuisine — from rich Mughlai feasts to South Indian spreads — prepared by our master chefs.

📸
Photography & Film

Cinematic videography and candid photography that captures every precious, fleeting moment of your day.

🎵
Music & Entertainment

Live bands, DJ setups, and dhol players to keep the celebrations vibrant from mehendi to bidaai.

🏨
Guest Accommodation

Partnered hotels nearby for out-of-town guests — comfortable stay packages available on request.

💄
Bridal Makeup & Styling

Renowned makeup artists and stylists for the complete bridal look — traditional to contemporary.

🚗
Transport & Valet

Vintage bridal cars, luxury baraat vehicles, and valet parking for all guests.

🕯️
Puja & Rituals

Expert pandit coordination for all pre-wedding and wedding-day ceremonies with Vedic traditions.
